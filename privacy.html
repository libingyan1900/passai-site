Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

PassAI ("we", "our", "us") operates the PassAI mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID, name (if you choose to share it), and email address (which may be a private relay address).

Study Progress: We store your quiz answers, scores, and study progress to provide personalized coaching and track your readiness.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized AI coaching and study recommendations
  • Sync your progress across devices
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Improve our services

AI Features

When you use the "Why?" explanation feature, your question context is sent to our AI service to generate personalized explanations. We do not use your data to train AI models.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS). Study progress is also cached locally on your device for offline access.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data from our servers.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Sign In: Authentication
  • Supabase: Data storage and backend services
  • xAI: AI-powered explanations
  • Apple StoreKit: In-app purchase processing

Children's Privacy

PassAI does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for users preparing for a driver's license exam.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub.
